Vote results are mapped by election-day polling division (does not include advance votes).

Mouse over a polling division to see vote results by candidate and party. Click to zoom in to the polling division.

Use TAB key to cycle through polling divisions in the currently selected riding.

Riding-level results are shown in tables below map. Click on the riding name to pan and zoom the map to that riding.
